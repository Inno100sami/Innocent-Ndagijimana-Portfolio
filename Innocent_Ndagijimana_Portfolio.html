Who I Am
About Me

I am a Registered Professional Engineer (IER Reg. A1093/EC/IER/2019) with over 10 years of multidisciplinary experience across civil engineering, water supply infrastructure, Non-Revenue Water (NRW) management, environmental GIS, and data science.

Currently serving as Operations Engineer at VEI B.V. (Dutch Water Operators) on the US Government-funded Thrive WASH – Isoko y'Ubuzima programme, I coordinate NRW reduction strategies and private operator capacity building across 10 districts in Rwanda.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge deep field engineering with strong project management and advanced data science — I build Python-based assessment tools and interactive HTML dashboards that transform raw operational data into management decisions. My GitHub (inno100sami) hosts tools actively used by rural water operators.

I hold three degrees including an MSc in Financial Engineering (WorldQuant University, ongoing) and an MSc in Environmental & Development Studies with specialisation in GIS.
